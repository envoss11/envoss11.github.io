I am a data analytics professional with over 5 years of experience in the financial services industry. I love helping businesses use data to ask more productive questions, solve problems and better serve their customers.

I am also a student in the [Statistics M.S. program](https://stat.tamu.edu/ms-statistics/) at Texas A&M with an empahsis in Applied Analytics & Data Science. In this program, I've developed deep knowledge of statistical theory and machine learning as well as extensive experience analyzing data and building models with R. 

I live in St. Paul, MN, along with my wife Jen and our 2 cats, Artemis and Apollo. When I'm not working or studying, you'll usually find me reading, listening to podcasts, or battling [beholders](https://forgottenrealms.fandom.com/wiki/Beholder) with friends in Dungeons & Dragons (virtually, these days).
